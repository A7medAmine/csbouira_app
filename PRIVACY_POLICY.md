# Privacy Policy — CS Bouira

**Last updated:** 28/06/2026

CS Bouira ("the App", "we", "us") is a free, non-commercial academic
resource-sharing application built for Computer Science students at
Université de Bouira, Algeria. This Privacy Policy explains what
information we collect, why we collect it, and what rights you have over
it.

This policy is written to comply in good faith with Algeria's Law No.
18-07 on the protection of natural persons in the processing of personal
data, as amended by Law No. 25-11 (2025).

## 1. Who is responsible for your data

CS Bouira is developed and operated by ABDESSELAM AHMED AMINE an independent
student developer, based in Medea, Algeria. For any privacy-related
question or request, contact: ahmedamineeabd@gmail.com.

## 2. What information we collect

We only collect what is needed to operate the App's features:

**If you create an account or sign in with Google:**

- Full name
- Email address
- A profile photo (only if you sign in with Google and choose to share
  it)

**If you upload a resource:**

- The full name and email address you enter on the upload form
- The file itself, and metadata about it (file name, type, the
  module/grade/semester you tagged it with)

**If you use the App as a guest (no account):**

- We do not collect or store any personal information server-side.
  Your favorites and recently-viewed items are stored only on your own
  device and are never sent to us unless you choose to create an account
  and sync them.

**Automatically, to make the App work:**

- Basic technical information needed to operate features (e.g. which
  files you've downloaded, stored only on your own device; your upload
  count, stored to show on the Leaderboard if you choose to upload while
  logged in).

We do **not** collect: your location, your contacts, your device's
advertising identifiers, or any data unrelated to the features described
above. We do not use any analytics or tracking SDKs.

## 3. Why we collect it

- **Account info (name, email, photo):** to let you log in, sync your
  favorites across devices, and show appropriate credit (name and
  avatar) on the Leaderboard if you choose to upload resources.
- **Uploaded file metadata:** to organize and display the resource
  correctly within the App's academic structure (year/semester/module/
  type).
- **Locally-stored guest data:** purely for your own convenience while
  using the App as a guest; this never leaves your device unless you
  choose to sync it by creating an account.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose
beyond operating the features described in this policy.

## 4. Where your data is stored

- Account data (name, email, favorites, upload records) is stored using
  **Supabase**, a third-party database service. Supabase may store data
  on servers located outside Algeria, depending on the hosting region
  configured for this project.
- Uploaded files are stored on **Google Drive**, via a Google Apps
  Script endpoint, as part of the existing CS Bouira resource-sharing
  system.
- If you sign in with Google, authentication is handled directly by
  Google; we never see or store your Google password.

Because some of this data may be processed or stored outside Algeria by
these third-party services (Supabase, Google), by using the App you
acknowledge this cross-border processing, which is necessary for the App
to function as a free service built on these platforms.

## 5. Your rights

Under Algerian Law No. 18-07, you have the right to:

- **Know** what data we hold about you (right to information).
- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correct** any inaccurate information (e.g. update your name).
- **Delete** your account and associated data.
- **Object** to processing of your data, including withdrawing consent
  at any time (note: withdrawing consent for required account features
  may mean you can no longer use those features, e.g. you can't sync
  favorites without an account).

To exercise any of these rights, contact ahmedamineeabd@gmail.com. We will
respond and act on reasonable requests as promptly as we can, given this
is a small, independently-run student project.

## 6. Deleting your account and data

You can request account deletion at any time by contacting ahmedamineeabd@gmail.com.
Once verified, we will delete your profile, favorites, and
upload records from our database. Files you previously uploaded to the
shared academic Drive may remain available to other students, since
they form part of the shared resource library the App exists to provide
— if you would like a specific uploaded file removed entirely, mention
this in your request and we will do so on a best-effort basis.

Guest-mode data stored only on your device can be cleared at any time by
clearing the App's local storage or uninstalling the App — we have no
access to this data and cannot delete it remotely since we never receive
it.

## 7. Children's privacy

CS Bouira is intended for university students and is not directed at
children. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age
typically associated with university enrollment.

## 8. Third-party services used by this App

| Service                    | Purpose                                   | Data involved                                  |
| -------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Supabase                   | Authentication, favorites, upload records | Name, email, avatar, app usage data            |
| Google Sign-In             | Login                                     | Name, email, profile photo                     |
| Google Drive / Apps Script | Hosting uploaded academic files           | Uploaded files, uploader name/email            |
| GitHub Releases            | App update distribution                   | No personal data — only checks version numbers |

We do not control these third parties' own privacy practices beyond
what's described here; we encourage you to review Google's and
Supabase's own privacy policies if you'd like further detail.

## 9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data (e.g. relying on
Supabase's built-in security features, including Row Level Security
policies restricting who can read or modify data). However, as with any
online service, we cannot guarantee absolute security, and you share
information with us at your own discretion.

## 10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves. Material changes will be
reflected in the "Last updated" date above, and significant changes will
be noted in the App's release notes.
